  • This patent forms part of Tesla's 'Unboxed' production method, an innovative approach where vehicle components are assembled individually before being combined in the final stage.

  • The patent details include a robotic station that uses vacuum clamps to secure parts, applies adhesive, and precisely aligns them using reference points.

  • The news was first revealed through industry influencers, with reports interpreting it as a key element in the Cybercab’s production process.
